Spud Counter

Goal:

Print the words to a song using a for loop and if statements to avoid duplicating print statements.

Steps:

  1. Optional: You can go to YouTube to hear the Potato Song.
  2. In your code, print the words to the song as shown below:

    1 potato, 2 potato, 3 potato, 4
    5 potato, 6 potato, 7 potato, more

  3. Your solution must contain: 
    for()
else if()
  4. Only one line of code must contain the word "potato" (and you cannot put it in a variable).
