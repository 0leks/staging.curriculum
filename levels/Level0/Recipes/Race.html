Race

Goal:

Draw two images. Press keys to make them race across space, then announce who is the winner!

Steps:

  1. Find the Race recipe program ( z_Race.pde ) and open it using Processing.
  2. Follow the instructions in the program to help you draw the racers and race them against each other. Use the racer images provided, or choose your own.
  3. Make sure you SAVE YOUR CODE when you are done.
