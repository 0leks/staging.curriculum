Tash Me


You are going to make a program that allows the user to put a mustache onto a face.

1. Create a new sketch in Processing and add setup() and draw() methods.

2. Find a photo of a face, it can be anyone you want:

  • Find an image on google
  • Right click on the image and select "Save Image As"
  • Rename your image something short (e.g. "face")
  • Save the image on your desktop
  • Drag the image from your desktop and drop it into your sketch


3. On line 1 of your sketch, add the following code:

PImage mustache;
PImage face;

4. Load your image by adding the following code to your setup() method:

face = loadImage("face.jpg");
size(800, 600);
face.resize(width,height);

5. If needed, change “face.jpg” from the previous step so that it matches the name of your image.

6. At the beginning of your draw() method, set friend as your background using the background() method.

7. Find an image of a mustache on google. Make sure it has a transparent background. Search for “mustache transparent” or “mustache png”. Repeat the instructions in step 2 to add it to your program.

8. In your setup()method, load your mustache image using the following code:

 mustache = loadImage("mustache.png");

9. If needed, change “mustache.png” from the previous step so that it matches the name of your image.

10. In your draw() method, add the mustache using:

image(mustache, 400, 300);

11. Change the previous line of code so that the mustache moves with your mouse (HINT: use mouseX and mouseY).

12. Use an if statement to make the mustache appear only when the mouse is pressed.

13. Inside of your if statement, redraw the background just like you did in the setup() method. This will make it so that only one mustache appears at a time.

OPTIONAL

14. Resize your mustache so that it fits better using:

 mustache.resize(w, h);

15. Add or subtract a number from mouseX and mouseY so that your mustache appears exactly where you click your mouse.
